Hi! I am Som Roy.

I am pursuing my final semester in Interaction Design at National Institue of Design, Bangalore. This summer I was at Google working as a UX Design intern, and I think I have gained a few extra pounds because the food there was so awesome! Last semester I spent my winters as an exchange student at Hogeschool voor de Kunsten Utrecht in a beautiful romanesque city of Netherlands.

After graduating in architecture, I joined Interaction Design to satiate my curiosity in emerging technologies and making them accessible for everyone. I like to work on real world problems and the fact that design can impact millions of lives just fascinates me.

Besides design I have huge love for food which treats me to carry around a few extra pounds. I like hanging out in old cities and, where I can find vintage buildings and interesting food joints. While at work, I play table tennis and drink "chai". Fun fact, I have lived in 8 till now, and currently I am temporarily settled in Bangalore.
