Hi I am Som.

This summer I was at Google working as a UX Design intern, and I think I have gained a few extra pounds because the food there was so awesome! Last semester I spent my winters as an exchange student at Hogeschool voor de Kunsten Utrecht in a beautiful romanesque city of Netherlands.
